Biography

Beyond This Place are melodic-hardcore/post-hardcore band from Lviv, formed in 2014. They released their debut studio single "Inner Deamon" on 4 June 2014. The band released their second studio track "Colliding (mixed by Ron Production Studio) on 9 November 2014. On 15-16 November 2014 they played their first show at "All Friends Tour" in Kamianets-Podilskyi and Khmelnytskyi. In April 2015, the band announced that vocalist Ruslan Bakhar has left the band. He was replaced by Stanislav Pashkalyan. They played two concerts on 5 September 2015 at "Respublica festival" and 30 October 2015 at Ivano-Frankivsk as support for a Space Of Variations. In mid-November 2015 Stanislav Pashkalyan announced that he would be leaving the band to pursue a career outside of music. After this, on May 2016 Andriy Dmytriv aslo decided to leave the band.

At this time, Beyond This Place are looking for a vocalist and bassist to restore their activity.
